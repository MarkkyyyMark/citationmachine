# Citation Machine — tester guide

Thanks for testing! This turns an article link + a quote into a Stoa evidence card.

## How to use it
1. Open the link you were sent.
2. Paste an **article URL** and the **exact quote** you want to cite.
3. Click **Build citation**. It fills in author, title, date, publisher, etc.
4. Click to **draft author credentials** (it searches the web for the lead author).
5. **Check every field** against the source — then copy the card into your doc.

## What I need from you
- **Did the card come out right?** Wrong format, wrong field, missing piece?
- **Credentials:** did they fill in? Were they correct, or a wrong guess?
  (Anything marked "UNVERIFIED" is the tool telling you to double-check — that's expected.)
- **Anything confusing, broken, or slow.** Which article link caused it (paste the URL).
- **Would you actually use this?** What would make it a yes?

## Known limits (don't report these)
- Credentials can take up to a minute (it's doing a live web search).
- Some paywalled/JS-heavy pages won't scrape cleanly — that's expected for now.
- Always verify before you use a card in a round. The tool never claims to be final.
